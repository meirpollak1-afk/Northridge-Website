Financing Structures

Loan Structures

StructureDescription
Acquisition Financing
Capital structured for the purchase of commercial real estate assets across all property types and investment strategies. Acquisition financing is tailored to the asset’s current performance, market dynamics, and the sponsor’s business plan, ensuring efficient execution and competitive terms.
Refinance
Refinancing solutions designed to replace existing debt and optimize a property’s capital structure. Borrowers may refinance to reduce borrowing costs, extend loan maturity, improve cash flow, or access equity for reinvestment, recapitalization, or portfolio growth.
Bridge Loans
Short-term, flexible financing used for transitional or time-sensitive situations. Bridge loans are commonly utilized for acquisitions, lease-up strategies, repositioning projects, or properties that require operational stabilization before securing permanent financing.
Construction Loans
Financing for ground-up development, major renovations, or significant property improvements. Construction loans are structured around project budgets, timelines, and sponsor experience, with funds typically disbursed through construction draws as the project progresses.
Permanent / Term Loans
Long-term financing designed for stabilized assets with predictable cash flow. Permanent loans provide borrowers with durable capital solutions, competitive interest rates, and structured repayment terms suited for long-term ownership.
CMBS Financing
Commercial Mortgage-Backed Securities (CMBS) loans offer fixed-rate, non-recourse financing that is securitized in the capital markets. These loans are often well-suited for stabilized commercial properties seeking longer terms, attractive pricing, and higher leverage.
Agency Financing (Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac / HUD)
Government-sponsored and government-backed loan programs primarily focused on multifamily assets. Agency financing offers attractive rates, longer amortization periods, and flexible structures for stabilized, workforce, and affordable housing properties.
